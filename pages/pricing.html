Questions

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The free trial requires no payment information. You'll only be asked for billing details when you choose to upgrade.

How do lifetime discounts work?

Founding Partners receive a permanent 50% discount on any paid plan. Early Adopters receive a permanent 25% discount. Both are applied to your account forever — they never expire, even as base prices change.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Proration is calculated automatically. Discount rates are preserved when you change plans.

What counts as a request?

One request equals one API call forwarded through the TOLVYN proxy to any AI provider — regardless of token count or model.
